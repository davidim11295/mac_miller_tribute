Malcolm James McCormick (Mac Miller)

Artist who touched millions even after death

Timeline of Malcolm James McCormick

  • 1992 - Born in Point Breeze, Pittsburgh.
  • 1998 - At the age of 6 he taught himself how to play piano, guitar, drums, and bass.
  • 2006 - At the age of 14 he Mac Miller started to learn how to rap.
  • 2006 - 2010 - He focused on rapping more than anything else.
  • 2010 - Graduated from Taylor Allderdice High School.
  • 2010 - Mac Miller signed with an independent label Rostrum Records, which leads to him creating the album K.I.D.S.
  • 2018 - Dies from drug overdose.
